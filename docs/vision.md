# AERIS Vision

AERIS is a mobile-first social platform focused on lightweight sharing, ephemeral Moments (Stories), real-time Chat, and AI-enhanced conversations via ERIZ AI.

Principles:
- Bottom navigation: Home / Chat / Account
- ERIZ AI lives inside Chat (pinned at top)
- Moments (Stories) live inside Home (horizontal row)
- Keep structure clean, scalable, and production-ready

Product notes:
- Home: Feed + Moments
- Chat: DMs, Circles, ERIZ AI
- Account: Profile, settings, buddies
